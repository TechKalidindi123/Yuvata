Programs

Services YUVA-TA offers

From celebration nights to career mentoring, every program is designed to strengthen skills, culture, and community responsibility.

Community & culture

  • Telugu festivals: Ugadi, Sankranti, Diwali celebrations
  • Cultural performances—dance, music, drama
  • Family meetups and networking
  • Community picnics and social gatherings
🎯

Youth leadership & development

Leadership workshops, public speaking and debate, student leadership councils, team building, and youth-led community projects.

❤️

Volunteering

Service events, food drives, environmental cleanups, event support, and volunteer hour certification.

🧭

Internships & service-learning

Nonprofit internships, community projects, school credit and service hours, and project-based learning.

💼

Careers & jobs

Job and internship listings, resume workshops, interview prep, professional mentoring, and networking events.

🫶

Support for low-income families

Food and clothing drives, school supplies, financial assistance pathways, and holiday support initiatives.

🎗️

Donations & fundraising

Online campaigns, charity events, sponsorships, and community fundraising drives.

🧠

Mental health awareness

Workshops, stress management, peer support groups, and guest speakers.

🌍

Disaster relief & healthcare support

Emergency fundraising, volunteer relief, healthcare awareness, and support for affected families.

👀

Shadowing opportunities

Job shadowing, career exploration days, industry exposure, and student–professional matching.

🌱

Mentorship

One-on-one mentoring, academic guidance, career planning, and college application support.

📜

Telugu heritage & education

Language classes, cultural history, traditional arts and crafts, and heritage awareness.

➗

Math tutoring

One-on-one and group tutoring, homework help, SAT and exam prep, beginner through advanced math, plus curated online tools on our Resources page.
