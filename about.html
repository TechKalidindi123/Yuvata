About us

Rooted in heritage. Rising toward tomorrow.

YUVA-TA is a nonprofit organization focused on youth development for young Telugu Americans. Our website is built to help families, youth, volunteers, donors, and neighbors learn about our mission, programs, resources, and ways to get involved.

What YUVA-TA means

YUVA-TA stands for Youth, Unity, Values, Awareness, Telugu Heritage, and Action. We create opportunities for leadership, education, service, cultural connection, and community growth.

Whether you are new to the area or have lived here for years, you belong. We celebrate Telugu language and traditions while preparing students to lead in school, work, and civic life.

Vision

To empower young Telugu Americans to become confident leaders, responsible citizens, and compassionate community members while staying connected to their Telugu culture and heritage.

Mission

YUVA-TA’s mission is to support youth development through education, volunteering, mentorship, cultural programs, leadership opportunities, mental health awareness, and community service.

Who we serve

Families and youth across our region

We serve families, students, young adults, and community members in Livingston, nearby cities, and North New Jersey—as well as friends and extended family connected to the Telugu community.

🏠

Families

Programs that welcome parents and siblings—from festivals to service days and learning support.

🎓

Students

Middle school through college: leadership tracks, tutoring, shadowing, and college guidance.

🙋

Volunteers & mentors

Professionals and community members who give time, skills, and encouragement to the next generation.
