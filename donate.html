Give

Fuel confident, caring leaders

Your donation powers cultural celebrations, leadership workshops, tutoring, mental health programming, and emergency support for families. YUVA-TA is grateful for every gift.

Make a gift

Choose an amount to start an email to our team with your intent. We will follow up with secure payment options (card, ACH, or check) and a tax acknowledgment.

When you are ready for instant online card processing, your administrator can drop in a Stripe, PayPal, Zeffy, or Give Lively link on this page.

Email to complete your donation

YUVA-TA is a nonprofit organization; consult a tax advisor regarding deductibility. EIN can be provided on request.

Your impact

  • $25 Supplies for one student leadership workshop
  • $75 Meals and materials for a community service outing
  • $150 Scholarship-style support for a family program fee
  • $500+ Sponsor a cultural performance or guest speaker series

Prefer to mail a check? Send payable to YUVA-TA to the address we provide when you email hello@yuva-ta.org.

Donations & fundraising programs
