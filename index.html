YUVA-TA is a nonprofit building community for young Telugu Americans—through culture, service, mentorship, and programs that help every student thrive.

Welcome

A home for families, students, and volunteers

We are building a welcoming, easy-to-navigate hub for families in Livingston, nearby cities, and across North New Jersey—plus friends and extended family connected to the Telugu community—so everyone can find programs, resources, and ways to get involved.

YUVA-TA stands for Youth, Unity, Values, Awareness, Telugu Heritage, and Action.

Explore programs

Vision

To empower young Telugu Americans to become confident leaders, responsible citizens, and compassionate community members while staying connected to Telugu culture and heritage.

Mission

We support youth development through education, volunteering, mentorship, cultural programs, leadership opportunities, mental health awareness, and community service.

What we do

Programs that meet youth where they are

From festivals and leadership labs to tutoring and disaster relief—YUVA-TA creates pathways for growth, service, and pride in heritage.

🪔

Culture & community

Ugadi, Sankranti, Diwali, performances, picnics, and family networking across North Jersey.

📣

Leadership & voice

Workshops, debate and public speaking, youth councils, and student-led service projects.

🤝

Volunteering & service

Food drives, cleanups, event support, and certified volunteer hours for school requirements.

📐

Academic support

Math tutoring, homework help, SAT prep, and curated learning links for every subject.

💚

Wellbeing & care

Mental health workshops, peer support, stress skills, and help for families in need.

🌏

Heritage & language

Telugu classes, history sessions, traditional arts, and heritage awareness for every age.
